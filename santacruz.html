Santa Cruz Motion Graphic

Motion Design

A nearly one-minute animation showcasing the traditions and culture of Santa Cruz de la Sierra, Bolivia. Inspired by real-life cultural elements like Carnaval, Chiquitania, and traditional folklore dances, I illustrated each scene in Illustrator and brought it to life with keyframe animation in After Effects.

Tools Used:

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop
